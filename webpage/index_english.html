Hotel Announcement

Food Safety of Papago International Resort complies with HACPP, so consumers don't have to worrying.
According to yesterday's announcement “Restaurant inspection plan for accommodation business” by the Food and Drug Administration of the Ministry of Health and Welfare, Papago International Resort is on the list of non-conformities. Papago conducted first sampling on September 9 2020, approved by review on October 15 2020; confirm that food safety is safe. Papago is the five-star hotel evaluated by the Tourism Bureau, and we have a responsibility to explain this to the outside world in order not to cause panic by misleading the public. To help travelers feel more secure about our food, Papago has also stated that it is only used local pigs, so consumers are encouraged to feel at ease. Best, Papago Headquarters.
